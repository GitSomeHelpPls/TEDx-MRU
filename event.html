Ideas Worth Spreading

The Beginning of Something Extraordinary

In 2024, TEDxMRU took its first bold step into the world of ideas worth spreading. Our inaugural event wasn't just a conference – it was a catalyst for change, a platform for innovation, and a celebration of human potential.

From technology pioneers to social innovators, from creative visionaries to scientific breakthroughs, our speakers shared stories that challenged perspectives and inspired action. Each talk was carefully curated to spark conversations that continue long after the curtains fell.

This is just the beginning. The foundation has been laid, the community has been ignited, and the stage is set for even more transformative experiences ahead.
