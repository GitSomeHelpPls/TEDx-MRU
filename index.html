Who We Are

Introducing TEDx MRU

TEDxMRU is an independently organized TED event at Manav Rachna University, showcasing inspiring speakers who share impactful ideas to ignite curiosity, foster creativity, and encourage meaningful conversations within the university and community.

About TED

"TED is a global platform dedicated to spreading ideas that matter. Originally conceived as a conference uniting Technology, Entertainment, and Design, TED has grown to encompass almost every topic under the sun, from science to business to global issues, in more than 100 languages. By sharing powerful ideas through short, impactful talks, TED bridges the gap between what is and what could be, igniting passion, curiosity, and action across the world."

About TEDx

"In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a grassroots initiative aimed at bringing TED like experiences to local communities. TEDx events are independently organized, fostering environments where innovative ideas can flourish and people can connect, question, and grow together. With TEDx, individuals around the globe have the chance to witness transformative ideas in their own communities, making complex topics accessible, inspiring change, and sparking curiosity."

About MRU

"Manav Rachna University (MRU) is a hub of knowledge, creativity, and transformation. Committed to fostering an environment of innovation, research, and societal impact, MRU is home to some of the brightest minds of tomorrow. We cultivate a culture of excellence that prepares students to lead, innovate, and make meaningful contributions to the world."

About TEDxMRU

"TEDxMRU is the premier stage where diverse voices, ground breaking ideas, and visionary thinking converge. Hosted by Manav Rachna University, TEDxMRU isn’t just a platform—it’s a community driven movement designed to Inspire, Ignite, and Innovate. We’re dedicated to creating a space where new ideas are shared, stories are told, and boundaries are pushed, making TEDxMRU a one of a kind experience."
